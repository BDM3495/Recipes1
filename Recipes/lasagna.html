Lasagna

Lasagna is delicous. It is nutritious. Kind of. Well it's food for the soul. Eat up with our recipe below!

Ingredients:

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Noodles
  • Patience and Determination

Steps:

  1. SAUCE! You need sauce. Add tomato sauce, paste and crushed tomatoes with ground beef, bell peppers and onions.

  2. NOODLES! Layers of noodles! Boil them and assemble your desired cheeses. Ricotta, Mozzarella and parmesean work best.

  3. ASSEMBLY! Layer noodles, some meat sauce and cheeses in a repeated process until the stack is as high as your oven can accommodate. The taller the stack, the better!

  4. BAKE! Just bake it. Depending on how tall your stack is, this may need to be done in sections. Dual ovens work best!
