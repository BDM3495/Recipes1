Eggs

Hard Boiled Eggs! What else is there to say?

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Fill pot with water and place eggs in.

  2. Bring pot of water and eggs to a roaring boil.

  3. Once boiling, turn off heat and cover for roughly 12 minutes.

  4. Remove eggs from hot water and place in cold water for 1 minute.

  5. Begin peeling and eating your hard boiled eggs!
